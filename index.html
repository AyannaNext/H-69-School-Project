School activity

Exams

There's Periodic Test 1 then Summer holiday for about a month. Then Periodic test 2, Half Yearlies, Periodic test 3 and then Winter holidays. Then Periodic test 4 and then final exams. Then you just repeat (for CBSE).



Sports day

Sports day usually occurs after Summer holidays in around July or August. There's various competitions like relay, three-legged race, a race with parents with their children, and dash race.



Annual day

Annual day occurs after the Winter holiday in January or February. Students from every class from nursery to around grade 6 or 8 have to dance on stage in front of the whole grades' parents and put makeup on.
